We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. However, due to the digital nature of MCP Power Pack (downloadable configuration files), the following refund policy applies.

## 2. Statutory Rights Notice

## 3. EU/UK Customers — 14-Day Cooling-Off Period
If you are located in the European Union or the United Kingdom, you have the right to withdraw from your purchase within **14 calendar days** without giving any reason.

### When the right of withdrawal is lost:
You expressly agree that the digital content will be supplied **before the 14-day period ends**, and **you acknowledge that once you download or access the Product, you lose your right of withdrawal**.

**You will be asked to confirm this at checkout by checking a box stating:**
> "I acknowledge that once I download the Product, I lose my 14-day right of withdrawal."

If you have NOT downloaded or accessed the Product within the 14-day period, you may request a full refund.

## 5. How to Request a Refund
1. Open an issue at: github.com/bambooshadow-studio/mcp-power-pack
2. Include your order email and order ID (found in your purchase receipt)
3. Describe the issue and the refund scenario you qualify under

We will respond within **7 business days**.

## 6. Refund Processing
- Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method
- Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days (depending on your payment provider)
- We will notify you when the refund has been issued
