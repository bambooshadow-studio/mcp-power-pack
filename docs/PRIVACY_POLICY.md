We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |

We require all third parties to respect your privacy and comply with applicable data protection laws.

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence (including the United States, where our service providers are located). When such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

## 6. Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Access controls for personal data
- Regular security assessments

## 7. Data Retention
We retain your data only as long as necessary:
- Order data: 7 years (tax/legal obligation)
- Account data: Until you request deletion
- Analytics data: 30 days (anonymized after)
- Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR)
If you are in the European Union or UK, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of the data we hold about you |
| **Rectification** | Correct inaccurate or incomplete data |
| **Erasure** | Request deletion of your data ("Right to be forgotten") |
| **Restriction** | Limit how we process your data |
| **Data Portability** | Receive your data in a machine-readable format |
| **Objection** | Object to processing based on legitimate interests |
| **Withdraw Consent** | Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing) |
| **Complaint** | File a complaint with your local data protection authority |

### To exercise any of these rights:
Open an issue at: github.com/bambooshadow-studio/mcp-power-pack

## 9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)
If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:
- **Right to Know**: Request details about data collected, used, or shared
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: We do NOT sell personal information, so no opt-out needed
- **Non-Discrimination**: Exercising your rights will not affect our service to you

## 10. Cookies

## 11. Children's Privacy
